Field Project - Mendix SAP Lab Assignment with the University of Oklahoma Price Business College

Team Member - Mendix programmer/Coordination

Aug 2020 - Dec 2020

Creating a Mendix Lab for the Electronic Business class within the Price College of Business. The lab will take students through how to create a mendix app that will produce information pertaining to the Procurement, Fulfillment, and another business cycle of their choice that the have learned in the Electronic Business class. the information will be taken from SAP. The format of the projects final output will be a lab assignment with prompts that the student follows along with including submission of gradable screenshots of the outputs form the business cycle.

Assistance League - Operation School Bell (MIS 3353 – Database Management) Spring 2020

Team Member - Database Creator/ Implementer and SQL Queries

Jan 2020 - May 2020

Worked with the Assistance League of Norman (Not Real Association) to evaluate how a new database can assist one of their main projects, Operation School Bell (OSB), with becoming more efficient in their endeavor to provide clothing to nearly 1600 financially disadvantaged children in local school districts. OSB’s primary goal for a new database is to have accurate and timely data of the project’s available inventories in order to help ensure the maximum number of students are helped each school year. The secondary goal is to provide efficiency and ease-of-use for management to run fast and accurate queries on data to help determine the amount of assistance provided and purchasing decisions for the fiscal year. Created junk data to make the tables, created the relationships (cardinalities) between them in SQL Server through a virtual desktop, and wrote out SQL queries to output requested data.
